ChofizzD 隱私權政策

最後更新日期：2026年7月8日

ChofizzD 非常重視您的隱私。本隱私權政策說明了本應用程式如何處理您的資料。

1. 資料收集

本應用程式致力於保護使用者的隱私。我們不會收集任何個人的身份識別資訊。您的運動紀錄、分數與相關資料均儲存於您的裝置本地端。

2. iCloud 同步

本 App 使用 Apple iCloud (CloudKit) 私有資料庫同步您的射擊賽紀錄、練習紀錄、地點與標籤等個人資料，僅用於在您登入相同 Apple ID 的多台裝置間同步顯示，資料儲存於您個人的 iCloud 帳戶中，開發者本身不會存取、蒐集或用於任何其他用途。您可隨時於裝置的「設定 > Apple ID > iCloud」中管理或關閉此 App 的 iCloud 同步權限。

3. 第三方服務

本應用程式不會將您的資料與任何第三方服務分享或用於廣告用途。

4. 隱私權保護措施

由於資料儲存於您的本地裝置或您私人的 iCloud 帳號中，我們建議您妥善保管您的裝置密碼與 Apple ID 帳號安全。

ChofizzD Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

ChofizzD highly values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how this application handles your data.

1. Data Collection

This application is committed to protecting user privacy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Your workout records, scores, and related data are stored locally on your device.

2. iCloud Synchronization

This app uses Apple iCloud (CloudKit) private database to synchronize your personal data, including shooting match records, practice records, locations, and tags. This is used solely to display and sync data across multiple devices logged into the same Apple ID. The data is stored in your personal iCloud account; the developer does not access, collect, or use it for any other purpose. You can manage or disable iCloud sync permissions for this app at any time by going to "Settings > Apple ID > iCloud" on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

This application does not share your data with any third-party services and does not use it for advertising purposes.

4. Privacy Protection Measures

Since your data is stored on your local device or your private iCloud account, we recommend that you properly secure your device password and Apple ID account.
